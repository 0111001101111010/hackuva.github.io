HackVirginia

24 Hours...

100+ students from all over Virginia

Sponsors will bring company representatives and engineers to collect participants’ resumes, judge the competition, and present career opportunities.

Free food, drinks, and coffee will be offered throughout the Hackathon.

Setting

Start: Friday, November 22
End: Saturday, November 23

Location: Clemons library, University of Virginia

Schedule

Friday, 4:00pm - Registration & Setup

Friday, 6:00pm - Start hacking!

Saturday, 6:00pm - Finish

Saturday, after finish - Judging & Closing

Theme

The HackVirginia team challenges you to build software that makes a difference in the community. Whether that be a mobile app, a web application, or some other form of software, it will be judged based on its novelty, breadth, and potential beneficial impact on the community.

Prizes!

Compete with your peers for a chance to win valuable prizes.

Thousands of dollars in prizes will be awarded to top 3 teams. Additionally, there will be door prize giveaways!

Teams

As long as your team does not exceed 5 participants, then you can work with anyone else who has registered.

We encourage you to reach out to your fellow hackers from other schools, as this is a great opportunity to make new friends and participate in a unique collaborative experience.

Things to bring

1) Yourself (required)

2) Laptops + chargers (pretty useful)

3) Ethernet cables (optional – WIFI will be available)

4) Sleeping supplies (optional – who needs to sleep anyway?)

Proceeds support a local charity

Computers4Kids

Proceeds from the hackathon will support mentorship and development of computer skills for low income, middle and high school students.
